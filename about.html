Our Journey

Company History

Unique Engineering & Consultancy Co. Ltd. was founded in 2011 and is headquartered in Juba, South Sudan. Over the past 15+ years we have grown into a trusted engineering and construction group delivering high‑quality projects across East Africa. Our sister company Unique Construction General Contractor (UCGC), registered in 2020 in Ethiopia, extends our reach and capabilities in the region.

We operate successfully in South Sudan, Ethiopia, Uganda, and Kenya, and are expanding to Western Africa very soon. Our combined teams include experienced engineers, consultants, project managers, and construction professionals committed to turning ideas into practical, sustainable, and innovative solutions.

From concept development and architectural planning to construction supervision and project execution, we provide comprehensive engineering services that meet international standards while addressing local needs. Our core strengths include professional engineers, quality assurance, on‑time delivery, and client satisfaction.

Our Mission

To deliver sustainable, high‑quality engineering and construction services that exceed client expectations, create value for stakeholders, and contribute to the development of Africa’s infrastructure.

Our Vision
